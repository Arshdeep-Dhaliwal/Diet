The Protocol - 9-Day OMD + 48-Hour Water Fast

An 11-day repeating cycle: nine OMAD days, then two water-fast days, then restart on Day 12.

Days 1-9 23-hour fast, 1-hour eating window, one large nutrient-dense evening meal, black coffee or water during the day, and fasted morning training.
Days 10-11 Water only plus electrolytes, light Zone 2 cardio only, no strength training, and black coffee or plain tea allowed.

Cycle Notes

  • The 11-day cycle repeats for 6 months. That is roughly 16 complete cycles.
  • The 48-hour fast days rotate through the week. Adjust training accordingly.
  • If a 48-hour fast falls on a strength day, swap to a light Zone 2 walk.
Breaking The 48-Hour Fast Start with warm bone broth or warm salt water, wait 30 minutes, then eat the regular OMAD meal. Eat slowly, chew thoroughly, and do not overeat.

Water Fast

48-Hour Water Fast - Days 10 and 11

After nine days of OMAD, the body is primed for a deeper fast with electrolytes, light movement, and a controlled refeed.

Intro Note The 48-hour fast triggers autophagy, resets insulin sensitivity, and accelerates fat loss. This is the Jamnadas protocol.

Day 10 Timeline

  • Last meal was Day 9 at 7:00 PM.
  • Morning: wake up, salt water, black coffee.
  • Training: light Zone 2 walk only for 30-45 min with HR under 120 bpm. No strength training.
  • Throughout the day: water plus electrolytes, with a pinch of salt in every glass.
  • Evening: herbal tea and magnesium before bed.

Day 11 Timeline

  • Morning: salt water and black coffee.
  • Training: light walk only or full rest. Listen to the body.
  • Afternoon: electrolyte water and rest.
  • 5:00 PM: break the fast with warm bone broth or warm salt water.
  • Wait 30 minutes, then eat the regular OMAD meal. Eat slowly, do not overeat, and keep portions smaller than usual.

Electrolytes During The Fast

  • Sodium: 1/2 tsp pink Himalayan salt in water, 2-3 times per day.
  • Potassium: Lite Salt or potassium supplement, 1/4 tsp per day.
  • Magnesium: 300-400mg magnesium bisglycinate before bed.

Allowed vs Not Allowed

  • Allowed: water, black coffee, plain green tea, herbal tea, electrolyte water, and apple cider vinegar in water.
  • Not allowed: any food, milk, cream, butter in coffee, chai with sugar, whey, collagen, fruit juice, coconut water, or any caloric drink.
