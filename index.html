Non-Negotiable Rules
1. Eat the same thing every day. No substitutions, no improvising.
2. Never skip breakfast protein. Your brain needs it for work.
3. Zone 2 = conversation pace. If you can't talk, slow down.
4. VO2 max intervals = go hard. 90–95% max HR. Suffer for 4 minutes, then recover.
5. Weigh yourself weekly (same day, morning, fasted). Expect 0.6–0.8 kg/week average.
6. Sleep 7+ hours. Non-negotiable. Fat loss stalls without sleep.
7. 8,000+ steps daily outside of gym sessions.
8. 3L water daily. More on workout days.
9. No alcohol. No juice. No caloric drinks except chai and milk as listed.
10. If you miss a workout, just do the next one. Don't double up.
